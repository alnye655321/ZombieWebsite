Zombie Home Invasion VR

Instruction Manual

A room-scale virtual reality game for the HTC Vive that places you in a post apocalyptic zombie world. Your goal is to defend a house from a zombie horde by manipulating your environment.

Game Objectives

  Details

Your goal is to outlast the zombie horde attack by any means neccesary. Winning means successfully defending the house and surviving for the allotted time. Default is 5 minutes

  Info

Manipulate your enviornment and explore. You will have all the setup time you need before the attack begins.

Zombies

  Details

The primary menace in the game. They are seemingly never ending and will try to end your day in a most gruesome way. Their primary objective is to tear through the windows of the house and then rush at you!

  Info

  • Zombies are hard to kill, headshots only! Best try to keep them out of the house to begin with...
  • If you must kill them using objects that you can pick up are a good start. However, killing will only delay the inevitable.
  • Zombies have a little brains left, they will attempt to surround you.

Windows

  Details

What the zombies will attempt to destroy to enter the house. Don’t they look menacing just beyond the window sill?

  Info

  • Windows have health which is displayed above them in game. Default start is 100 health with 1 board and 4 nails.
  • Add boards and nails to windows to buff its health up to a max of 200. Each window can accept an additional 2 boards and 4 nails per board.
  • Each successful zombie strike on a window will reduce its health by 1. Boards and nails will start to fall off. You may replace them through your exceptional carpentry skills.
  • When groups of zombies are attacking one window they can come down fast.
  • At 0 health the window is destroyed and the zombies start pouring in.

Walkie Talkie

  Details

The last means left of communication. Reliable up to approximately 3 miles.

  Info

Used to trigger the start of the zombie attack. You will receive an unexpected call…

Boards

  Details

Your standard life-saving 2 by 4.

  Info

Use these to fix in windows to keep those nasty zombies out of your house.

Game Clock

  Details

Your everyday digital clock display counting down the seconds your life ends.

  Info

Keep it with you or toss it away.

Crowbar

  Details

Half-life anybody :)

  Info

Use it to pry boards off of windows so they can be moved where needed.

Interactive Objects

  Details

Touching and feeling is okay

  Info

Lots of objects in the world can be manipulated, some to your great advantage.

Hammer

  Details

A zombie fighting carpenters dream.

  Info

May be used to hammer nails into boards. Main tool to buff a window’s health.

Nails

  Details

Don’t step on them, they look rusty!

  Info

Carry them over to boards and keep your hammer handy.
