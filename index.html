Zombie Home Invasion VR

Castle Doctrine Zombie Style

A room-scale virtual reality game for the HTC Vive that places you in a post apocalyptic zombie world. Your goal is to defend a house from a zombie horde by manipulating your environment.

Development

This game was my final project for the Galvanize Full Stack Immersive Course in Denver, CO. The bulk of the game was developed over the course of a crazy 2 week period. It was also my first Unreal Engine 4 project.

At first I didn’t know whether I would enjoy the experience of developing in VR. While it added additional challenges it turned into a great learning adventure. The experience gave me a new appreciation of the possibilities of VR and its awe inspiring to know that we are just scratching the surface.

I hope continue to work on the project in the coming months and prepare for an eventual release on Steam Greenlight. Stay tuned for updates. I hope you enjoy the game as much as I’ve enjoyed making it!

- Alex Nye
